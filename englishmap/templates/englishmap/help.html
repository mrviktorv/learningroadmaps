Help / FAQ

Q: I don't understand some words!

Try using Google translate.
OR
Try installing Google Translate plugin.

Q: I don't understand anything, help!

It can be hard for beginners to study on their own, try to find someone who has experience with English and ask them to explain you the basics.

Q: How can I search for a topic on the map?

Try pressing "ctrl" + "f" (on Windows) or "cmd" + "f" (on Mac) on your keyboard to open the search bar.
