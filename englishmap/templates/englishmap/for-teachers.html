Some useful sources for teachers:

Books are great, but in the 21 century, web sources have taken a major role in education, + they are usually much more affordable than books and are great for online studying/teaching. There are a plenty of websites with exercises, tests, worksheets, etc. out there, these are some that we use and recommend:

  • Test-english.com

    Here you can find lots of grammar tests.

  • British Council

    Serious materials for every possible topic, I guess.

  • ISLcollective

    Worksheets and activities. By teachers, for teachers.

  • Grammar Monster

    Thoroughly comprehensive list of grammar terms, test, exercises.
